How to Play

PhillyTap is a daily location guessing game featuring 5 new locations in Philadelphia every day.

Objective: Guess the location of each place on the map.

  • Tap anywhere on the map to make your guess
  • Confirm your guess to see how close you were
  • Closer guesses earn more points (up to 100 points per location)
  • Complete all 5 locations to see your final score

Scoring:

  • Base score: 0-100 points based on distance from the actual location
  • Location 3 gets 2√ó multiplier
  • Locations 4-5 get 3√ó multiplier
  • Maximum possible score: 1000 points

Daily Challenge: New locations are added every day, so come back tomorrow for a fresh challenge!
